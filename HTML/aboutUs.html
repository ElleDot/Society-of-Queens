Sarah Orola

Co-Founder

Travel. Poetry. Empowerment.
That is Sarah in a nutshell.

I heard once that every mind is a creative soul, and I strongly believe that. I co-founded a female collective, the Society of Queens, alongside my sister to create a network for young girls. I hope to empower all young women, especially girls like me, growing up in today's society to achieve the unthinkable! Reach me at: orola.sarah@gmail.com

Angel Orola

Co-Founder

Hey Queens, I'm Angel. I co-founded the Society of Queens with Sarah because I wanted to offer you a place for growth where women empower other young women, one where we learn what it means to be young, free and female in our generation.

As a 19-year-old, I myself am also learning so we might as well learn together with one another! I’m university studying Politics and Economics, believing that if we look at things from a feminist viewpoint, different, mutually beneficial outcomes will arise for men and women alike.
